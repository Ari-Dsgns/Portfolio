about me

I'm passionate of fine designs and meticulous of symmetry so that the forces are compensated to provide stability to the observer, although a touch of madness reminds us We are human.

Over time, I have found the balance between designing and programming. Indeed I've found in the middle of both a kind of shelter where I can let my imagination run wild.

"Below the surface of the machine, the program moves. Without effort, it expands and contracts. In great harmony, electrons scatter and regroup.
The forms on the monitor are but ripples on the water. The essence stays invisibly below"
